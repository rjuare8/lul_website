Mission

La Unidad Latina, Lambda Upsilon Lambda Fraternity, Incorporated primarily seeks to take a leadership role in meeting the needs of the Latino community through academic achievement, cultural awareness, community service and promotion of the Latino culture and people.

Hispanic Heritage Month BBQ

We host our annual BBQ at the beginning of Hispanic Heritage Month. For this event, we invite all LCASO organizations to bring dishes that represent Latin American cuisine. Attendees will enjoy a variety of foods and live performances from a grupo and a DJ that celebrate the rich Latino culture.

×

Women's Empowerment Dinner

The event brought together students and featured insightful presentations by three distinguished UIUC women faculty members, who discussed the importance of Women's History Month and empowerment.

×

H.O.P.E Gala

We host our annual gala to raise funds for the I-HOPE scholarship, awarded annually to four undocumented students. The evening will feature performances, delicious food, educational activities, and speeches that highlight the undocumented experience.
